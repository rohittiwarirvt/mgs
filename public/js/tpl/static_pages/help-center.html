Help Center

1. Are the professionals/technicians well qualified?

Yes. We only have professionals who have necessary qualification & experience and been recommended and certified by experts. We conduct interview, check referrals for all the professionals/technicians.

2. What happens if the professional/technician does not arrive on time?

We will arrange for a replacement professional and work with you to schedule a visit convenient with you to do the job or you can also cancel the service requested.

3. What happens in case the requested job takes more time than expected?

We at Mygharseva understand that your time is valuable and we are determined to limit the time spent by professional at your place. If the time for service is going to be more than planned, the professional will communicate the same to you.

4. Do you provide service on public holidays?

Yes we do on all holidays except national holidays. Our services are available everyday* from 10 AM to 8 PM *Note: Except for some uncertain circumstance which may interrupt our functioning.

5. How can I pay for my services on Mygharseva ?

You can login to our website and make a payment using your debit or credit card, or through net banking.

6. How many cities Mygharseva operates in?

We have started the service in Pune. We will soon expand our service in 4 more cities.

7. What happens if the service provider breaks/damages anything?

All of our service providers have passed through a rigorous assessment process. In the unlikely event of them breaking something we provide an insurance claim of up to Rs.5000.

8. If raw material/ extra supplies are required, will the technician bring it?
